Install on your phone
1
Open this page in Chrome on your Android device.
2
Tap the ⋮ menu (three dots) in the top-right corner of Chrome.
3
Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app" from the menu.
4
Confirm by tapping "Add" or "Install". The app icon will appear on your home screen.
💡 A banner may also appear automatically at the bottom of the screen — tap it to install directly.
1
Open this page in Safari on your iPhone or iPad.
2
Tap the Share button (the square with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom of Safari.
3
Scroll down in the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen".
4
Tap "Add" in the top-right corner. The app icon will appear on your home screen.
💡 The app must be opened in Safari — Chrome and other browsers on iOS do not support "Add to Home Screen" as a PWA.
